In 2010, ETA Company was founded in Gaza City, Palestine, by Abdel Salam Yassin, who dreamt of changing the world through technology. The company started by providing IT services to small and medium-sized enterprises and quickly gained a reputation for high-quality services and excellent customer support. In 2012, ETA decided to expand its operations to include app development. The company launched its first app, a social networking app called "Connect," which was a huge success and received thousands of downloads. This success encouraged the company to continue developing apps, and it launched many other apps in the following years, including gaming, education, and e-commerce apps. By 2015, ETA had become one of the leading app development companies in Palestine. The company continued to grow and expand, hiring more employees and opening new offices in other cities. The company also started working with international clients, including major companies in the United States and Europe.
